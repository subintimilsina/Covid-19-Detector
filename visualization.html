We have employed Deep Learning techniques with deep CNN based models for the detection of corona virus as well as pneumonia infected patient using CRX radio graphs. Grad-CAM has been used for reasoning to show the level of transparency making our predictions more precise and visually readable.



Grad-CAM algorithm relies on the fact that model has detected some region of image that brought the classification factor of the Chest X-rays. Grad-CAM algorithm is based on the forward propagation and the backward propagation.
